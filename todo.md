# Remaining Work

- `ngon_k_fold_iff` closes the full program at every tool strength: for every k and every n ≥ 3, cos(2π/n) lies in `OrigamiNumK k` exactly when every prime factor of φ(n) is at most 2k+1.
- `k_fold_lill` generalizes the two- and three-crease Lill systems to k coupled mirrors solving the degree-(2k+1) Bring-Jerrard trinomial, and the general-coefficient Lill chain over arbitrary coefficient lines removes the trinomial restriction.
- An upper bound for the full simultaneous two-fold alignment catalog — that no pair of coupled creases under any Alperin-Lang alignment produces a number outside `OrigamiNum2` — is formalized as an elimination-degree bound over the catalog, the open completeness problem of the field.
